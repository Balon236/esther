General Pet Care Tips

  • Provide clean, fresh water at all times
  • Feed a balanced, species-appropriate diet
  • Regular veterinary check-ups (at least annually)
  • Proper grooming and hygiene
  • Safe, comfortable living environment
  • Mental stimulation and exercise

Watch for these common pet health problems:

  • Dogs: Fleas/ticks, ear infections, arthritis, dental disease
  • Cats: Urinary tract issues, hairballs, kidney disease
  • Birds: Respiratory infections, feather plucking, nutritional deficiencies
  • Small mammals: Dental overgrowth, digestive issues, respiratory infections
