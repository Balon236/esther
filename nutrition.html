Animal Nutrition Guide

Feeding recommendations and nutritional requirements for different animals

Nutritional Requirements

Proper nutrition is essential for animal health, growth, reproduction, and productivity. Requirements vary by species, age, and purpose.

Macronutrients

  • Proteins: For growth and repair
  • Carbohydrates: Energy source
  • Fats: Concentrated energy, essential fatty acids

Micronutrients

  • Vitamins: Various metabolic functions
  • Minerals: Bone formation, enzyme functions
  • Water: Most critical nutrient

Feeding Recommendations

Animal Daily Food Requirement Key Nutrients Feeding Tips
Adult Dog 2-3% of body weight Protein (18-25%), fats (10-15%) Divide into 2 meals, provide fresh water
Dairy Cow 3-4% of body weight (dry matter) Fiber, protein (16-18%), calcium Balance forage and concentrate, adjust for lactation
Layer Hen 100-120g/day Protein (16-18%), calcium (3.5-4%) Provide oyster shell separately, fresh water always
